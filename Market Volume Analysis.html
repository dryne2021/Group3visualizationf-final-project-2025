Market Volume Analysis 2013-2018

Historical Context: This visualization shows the explosive growth of cryptocurrency trading volume during crypto's formative years. From modest beginnings in 2013 with primarily Bitcoin trading, the market expanded dramatically through 2017's bull market, with Ethereum and altcoins gaining significant volume share. The visualization captures key events like Mt. Gox collapse (2014), Ethereum launch (2015), and the 2017 altcoin season.

Historical Insight: Notice the exponential growth pattern starting in 2016, leading to the 2017 parabolic move. The market shows maturation with increasing volume diversity as Ethereum and altcoins gain prominence.

2013-2018 Volume Pattern Insights

Early Market Patterns (2013-2015)

  • 2013: Bitcoin dominated (95% volume)
  • 2014: Mt. Gox collapse reduced volume 70%
  • 2015: Ethereum launch began diversification
  • Early institutional interest emerged

Bull Market Events (2016-2017)

  • 2016: Bitcoin halving sparked interest
  • 2017: ICO boom drove altcoin volume
  • Mainstream media coverage increased participation
  • Exchange proliferation expanded access

Market Maturation (2018)

  • Q1 2018: Volume declined 60% from peak
  • BTC dominance recovered to 50%+
  • Volume became more evenly distributed
  • 24/7 global trading established as norm
