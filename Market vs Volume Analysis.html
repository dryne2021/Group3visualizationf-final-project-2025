2013-2018 Market Evolution Summary

The 2013-2018 period shows cryptocurrency market maturation through strengthening market cap-volume correlation. From Bitcoin's early monopoly (0.45 correlation) to post-2017 correction efficiency (0.72 correlation), the market evolved from speculative chaos to more rational pricing. The 2017 bull market created a cluster of assets with disproportionately high volume relative to market cap, representing speculative excess that was largely filtered out by the 2018 correction.
