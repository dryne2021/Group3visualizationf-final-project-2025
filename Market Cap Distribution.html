Understanding Pie Charts

Pie charts effectively communicate proportional relationships by dividing a circle into sectors. In this visualization, each sector represents a cryptocurrency's market share percentage. Larger sectors indicate higher market capitalization, while color coding helps distinguish between different assets for easy comparison.

Market Structure Health Assessment

The current market structure shows moderate concentration with Bitcoin maintaining just over half of total market capitalization. While still concentrated, this represents significant progress from the extreme concentration (>90% Bitcoin dominance) seen in earlier years. The Herfindahl Index of 0.38 indicates a moderately concentrated market that is gradually decentralizing. A healthy target range would be 0.25-0.35, suggesting the market is moving in the right direction but still has room for further diversification.
