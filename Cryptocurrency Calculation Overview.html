Core Calculation Framework

Our calculations incorporate both fundamental and technical analysis metrics, weighted by market significance and predictive validity. Each metric undergoes normalization and standardization processes to ensure comparability across different cryptocurrencies.

Weighted Performance Score

Composite score combining volume, price stability, and market cap with proprietary weighting algorithms.

Score = Σ(metricᵢ × weightᵢ) ÷ Σ(weightᵢ)

Volatility-Adjusted Return

Return metrics normalized by volatility to identify risk-efficient investment opportunities.

VAR = Return ÷ (σ × √time)

Liquidity Index

Measures trading depth and market efficiency based on volume-to-spread ratios.

LI = Volume ÷ (Spread % × 100)

Momentum Indicator

Calculates price momentum across multiple timeframes to identify trend strength.

MI = (Priceₜ - Priceₜ₋ₙ) ÷ Priceₜ₋ₙ

Dynamic Weighting System

Our proprietary weighting algorithm assigns dynamic weights to different metrics based on market conditions, historical significance, and predictive power. Weights are recalculated quarterly using machine learning models that analyze performance correlation with future returns.

  • Volume Metrics: 25-35% weight depending on market phase
  • Price Stability: 20-30% weight with inverse correlation to volatility
  • Market Indicators: 15-25% weight based on market cap tier
  • Technical Signals: 20-30% weight adjusted for timeframe

Advanced Normalization Techniques

We employ min-max scaling, z-score normalization, and robust scaling techniques to ensure comparability across cryptocurrencies with different scales and distributions. Outliers are handled using IQR-based winsorization to maintain data integrity.

Composite Score Construction

Composite scores are calculated using weighted geometric means to better capture relative performance. Scores are then normalized to a 0-100 scale for easier interpretation, with periodic calibration against market benchmarks.

Validation & Backtesting

All calculation methodologies undergo rigorous backtesting using historical data from 2013-present. We employ cross-validation techniques and compare against benchmark indices to ensure predictive validity and robustness.
